Tonkatsu Recipe

Tonkatsu

Tonkatsu, which translates to fried pork, is definitely one of the trademark foods many restaurants proud themselves of.

Ingredients:

  • Pork cutlet-------------x1
  • Egg---------------------x3
  • All purpose flour-------depends
  • Panko(Bread crumbs)-----depends
  • Frying oil like canola
  • Worcestershire sauce----2 tbsp
  • Ketchup-----------------3tbsp

Steps:

  1. Without cutting pok cutlet, coat pork with flour
  2. Then dip pork into eggwash
  3. Finally cover pork with panko
  4. Fill pot with some oil, enough to fully submerge the pork cutlet. Heat oil to 325 F, then deep fry until brown.
  5. Put cutlet onto kitchen paper towel to soak up excess oil
  6. Mix worcestershire sauce and ketchup together for dipping
  7. Plate!
