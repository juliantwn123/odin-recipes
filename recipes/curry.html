Curry Recipe

Japnese Curry

Japanese curry is one of the most famous food amongst adults and chldren alike. Most curry sauce already came pre-packaged, so it's a very fast and easy way to make a delicious dish!

Ingredients:

  • Rice--------------1.5 cups
  • Potato------------x2
  • Carrot------------x1
  • Onion-------------x0.5
  • Curry cube--------Half of the package

Steps:

  1. Cook 1.5 cups of rice in a rice cooker
  2. Cut carrot into bite size
  3. Cut potatos into chunk size, as smaller pieces tend to dissolve
  4. Dice the onion, a bit bigger is fine
  5. Throw all cut ingredients into a pot on medium heat for a bit. No seasoning required.
  6. Fill pot to just a bit below the top most ingredients, too much water results in runny sauce
  7. Let ingredients boil for about 20 ~ 30 min, judging buy softness of ingredienes. Carrot and potato should be easily mushed by a spoon
  8. Throw curry cube into pot, let it dissolve
  9. Plate!
