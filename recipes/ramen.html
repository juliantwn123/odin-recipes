Ramen Recipe

Ramen

Everyone knows ramen, a simple dish with soup and noodles. But what makes a good ramen is in the broth!

Ingredients:

  • Chicken stock---------700ml
  • Water-----------------300ml
  • Soy sauce-------------4 tbsp
  • Sesame oil------------2tsp
  • Garlic----------------3 cloves
  • Ramen noodles---------400g
  • Sliced roasted pork---400g

Steps:

  1. Mix stock, water, soy sauce, and sesame oil together, and simmer for 5 min
  2. Cook the noodles according to instruction
  3. Plate with pork on top, in addition can add seaweed slices or boiled egg
